Nintendo

(Intro)

a Japanese multinational consumer electronics and video game company headquartered in Kyoto. Nintendo is one of the world's largest video game companies by market capitalization, creating some of the best-known and top-selling video game franchises. the company had tried several small niche businesses, such as cab services and love hotels.
